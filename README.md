## üí° Core Concepts & Topics

This course covers a wide range of foundational ECE topics:

**Circuit Fundamentals:**
* Node Voltage Method
* Thevenin & Norton Equivalents
* Superposition
* Feedback & Op-Amp Golden Rules

**Solid-State Devices:**
* Diodes and Capacitors
* MOSFET Physics & Operating Regions
* Small Signal Analysis
* Common-Source Amplifiers

**Frequency Domain Analysis:**
* Fourier Series
* Log Plots (Bode Plots)
* Transfer Functions
* Complex Impedance
* AC Power

**Power Electronics:**
* DC-DC Buck Converter Design
